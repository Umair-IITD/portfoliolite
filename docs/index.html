Questions

Honest answers

Is my financial data really private?
Yes, completely. PortfolioLite stores everything in SQLite on your device. We have no server that receives your data. There is no account, no sync, no cloud backup. The privacy policy is one page and says exactly this — we have nothing to store because nothing leaves your phone.
What happens if I lose my phone?
Your data is on your device, so a factory reset or lost phone means the data is gone. This is the honest trade-off of true privacy. We recommend using the CSV export feature regularly as a backup — save it to Google Drive or email it to yourself. A cloud backup option (encrypted, optional) is planned for a future version.
Does it connect to Zerodha, Groww, or my bank?
No, and that is intentional. PortfolioLite is manual-entry only. You type in your asset values. This means no account linking, no credentials, no API tokens. It takes 30 seconds to add an asset. The trade-off is real-time prices are not automatic — you update values when you want to.
Can I pay via UPI — PhonePe, GPay, Paytm?
Yes. We support payment via UPI (₹45) in addition to Google Play Billing (₹49). The UPI option is available directly from the app's payment screen at launch.
When does it launch?
Android launch is planned for Q2 2026. Join the beta list above and you will be the first to know — we will email you the Play Store link the moment it goes live.
